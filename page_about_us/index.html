OUR STORY

Duheri is an online footwear brand that is working towards reviving the age-old craft, training the artisans on modern processes, diversification, marketing the products to the customers and much more.With a history dating back to about 400 years, Kolhapuri chappals have always been one of the favourites of not just the Maharajas but also the local folk. Now, with a vision to restore the lost glory of Kolhapuri chappals with a modern touch, Ms. Ragini M Deo, Founder and Managing Director, Duheri, set up her own venture to promote the craft along with enabling sustainable livelihood for the Kolhapuri artisans.
